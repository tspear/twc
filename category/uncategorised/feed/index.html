Woah! A new Godspeed album arrived out of the blue (again). Titled “Asunder, Sweet and Other Distress” – it appears to be a recording of the 45 minute ‘track’ they have been playing at live shows for a year or so. This track had a working title of ‘Behemoth’ but it was unclear whether it was a full track or several pieces joined together. Well now we know….
