It doesnt seem like 20 years since the Exile on Mainstream label started life, time moves quickly…

In April this year the label will be celebrating its 20th Birthday in fine style at arguably our favourite venue in the world, the magnificent UT Connewitz in Leipzig.

We are very excited to be taking part in the event, we’ll be playing on Thursday April 4 at the nearby chapel, with our friends A WHISPER IN THE NOISE, with whom we played over 10 years ago!

Please come and join us for an amazing 4 days of LOVE, NOISE and FREEDOM!
